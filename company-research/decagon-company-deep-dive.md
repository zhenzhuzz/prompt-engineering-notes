## Why Decagon Matters

Decagon represents a new breed of AI company: **built by founders who already had successful exits, targeting an enterprise pain point with measurable ROI, and growing at unprecedented speed**.

> "75% of consumers reported that chatbots couldn't handle complex questions" â€” This was the insight that drove Decagon's founding.

**The Structural Advantage**: Customer support is the ideal first use case for AI agents because:
1. Enterprises already have human support infrastructure as a safety net
2. ROI is quantifiable (CSAT, resolution rates, labor cost savings)
3. The economic incentive is massive (BPO industry is $300B+)

**Key Insight for AI Builders**: While most AI startups chase horizontal platforms, Decagon went deep on one vertical first â€” and that focus enabled them to outperform giants like Salesforce in head-to-head evaluations.
